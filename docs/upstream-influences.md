The finite-sample and distributed promotion gates are FP design inferences assembled from these observed behaviors; no upstream claims to provide statistical generalization or model-weight training. Two to four independent positive cases therefore use leave-one-case-out, while negative controls, invariants, shadow observations, approval, source freshness, and rollback remain separate zero-tolerance gates.

These are audit baselines, not automatic update channels. Re-audit a newer revision before claiming that a rule reflects current upstream behavior. License and provenance notes are in [THIRD_PARTY_NOTICES.md](../THIRD_PARTY_NOTICES.md).
